Transition Properties:

  • transition-duration
  • transition-property
  • transition-timing-function
  • transition-delay

transition-delay

Specifies delay of the transition.

  • transition-delay: 1s;
  • transition-delay: 0.5s;
  • transition-delay: 2ms, 4ms;

The order of transition-duration and transition-delays fits the order of transition-properties.

.animated {
transition-property: background-color, border-radius, transform;
transition-duration: 2s, 6s, 1s;
transition-delay: 1.5s, 3s, 5s;
}
